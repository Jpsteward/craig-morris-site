Advantages of Pure Water Window Cleaning

Improved Results. Pure water actually gets windows cleaner than traditional methods and windows stay that way for longer. All frames and sills are washed each visit.


Health and Safety. Health and safety at work is a big issue, particularly when the use of ladders is involved. Pure water window cleaning or "Reach and wash" greatly reduces risk of injury, damage to your property and complies with the Work at Height Regulations 2005.


Difficult To Reach Windows. Access with the Pure Water Reach And Wash System has been dramatically improved. Those hard to reach windows that traditional window cleaning methods struggle with are no longer a problem, i.e above a conservatory roof, velux or second story windows.


Gutter Vacuuming

We clean from the ground using powerful vacuum cleaner and lightweight carbon fibre poles. We can work up to a height of 9m and can safely clean over conservatories and fragile roofs.
